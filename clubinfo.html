Club Information

Welcome to the official site of Hyderabad Anime Club. Currently there are 100+ members in our club and we are searching for more members to grow our club. Our only aim is to grow the club like other big anime clubs like Mumbai, Delhi etc. Right now India needs anime and we are desperately fighting for anime in India. We had a great success in 2019 by the anime movie (Tenki no Ko) release in theatre all over India due to the Indian otaku support we were able to make it a huge success and again in 2020 we had another anime movie in India. This shows the progression of anime in India. In order to grow the anime fan base throughout India, anime clubs were made for every state by the help of @AnimeTMtalks and even other YouTubers who helped the anime to rise in India. We thank and support them all. In order to create a strong fan base in Hyderabad, we need to grow this club.
